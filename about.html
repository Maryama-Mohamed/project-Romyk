About Icecream

Ice cream is a sweet, frozen dessert made from dairy products like milk and cream, combined with sugar and flavorings. It is churned while freezing to incorporate air and prevent ice crystals from forming, which gives it a smooth, creamy texture While delicious, ice cream is often high in sugar and fat, so it's best enjoyed in moderation or with healthier alternatives like frozen banana blends or low-sugar versions.
